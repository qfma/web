Fabian Zimmer

Bioinformatics & Computational Biology

I am a final year PhD student in the group of Prof Judith Mank at University College London (UCL) and expect to graduate in summer 2016. My work is on the interface of evolutionary biology and bioinformatics: I develop computational tools to answer fundamental biological questions. I am experienced working with complex and heterogeneous datasets and enjoy building tools in a diverse and international team. My ability to solve difficult problems and communicate results to others has been recognised with multiple awards.
